The conversion loop

Advertising that talks back

LocalShoppie isn’t a one‑off ad. It’s a two‑way WhatsApp channel that keeps suggesting, nudging and selling long after the customer leaves your store.

  • Suggest — AI recommends pieces from your store that suit each shopper.
  • Notify — well‑timed messages on new drops, restocks and occasion picks.
  • Convert — one‑tap buy links turn a suggestion into a checkout, in‑chat.
  • Learn — every interaction sharpens the next recommendation.
